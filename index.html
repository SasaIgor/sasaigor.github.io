Welcome to Friends of Seaview Pier

Friends of Seaview Pier is an organization devoted to the restoration and upkeep of a pier in the town. A pier is a wooden structure that provides a walkway over the sea. The pier requires regular maintenance, and the friends of the pier need to raise money for this purpose.

About Us

Friends of Seaview Pier is committed to preserving and maintaining the beauty of our beloved pier. We believe in community involvement and volunteer support to achieve our goals.

Membership

Members of Friends of Seaview Pier contribute $75 per year to support the pier's maintenance. This entitles them to free admission to the pier throughout the year. They can also volunteer to help run the pier by working at the pier entrance gate, working in the gift shop, or assisting with painting and decorating.
